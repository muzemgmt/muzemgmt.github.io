About

Based out of Milwaukee, Wisconsin, DJ, producer, and artist Nick Milton, has been making waves in the Midwest electronic scene as the bass-heavy project, OUTAGE. In less than a year, his breakout bootlegs and originals have garnered tens of thousands of plays on Soundcloud, while also earning the support of bass music pioneers, Zeds Dead. OUTAGE is on a meteoric rise, taking elements of underground and modern dubstep to create insanely high energy original songs and sets, pushing boundaries, and redefining what live bass music sounds like. A solid brand identity, strategic direct support slots for heavyweights like Virtual Riot, Gentlemens Club, and PhaseOne, along with his debut festival appearance at Dancefestopia, has solidified himself as the one to watch in the bass music circuit.
